Who are we?

We are a first year group of students from the Alpha class. We have been voted and we are proud to represent you.

If we are here, it's because of you, so don't hesitate to come and see us. We will try to help you as much as we can. You can be asking for help on languages but also for a living place or even food.

Remember that where there is a will, there is a way and We, the Algosup Student Council will proudly be helping you follow your way.

You will find bellow the names of the members. Also, the ASC will always accept volunteers and advices. This is always benefic to have refreshing ideas!
